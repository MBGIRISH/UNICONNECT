📍 ANSWER TO YOUR QUESTION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: "if i want to add events/hackathons of other college will it 
    not effect tell me."

A: NO, IT WILL NOT NEGATIVELY AFFECT! ✅

Why:
• Events are clearly labeled with college badges (🎓 IIT Delhi)
• Students can see which college hosts each event
• Inter-college events are GOOD for engagement
• Students discover opportunities beyond their campus
• You can add filtering later: "My College" vs "All Colleges"

Example:
┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
│ Tech Fest 2025                      │
│ 🎓 IIT Delhi (Your College)        │ ← On campus
│ 👥 142 going                       │
└─────────────────────────────────────┘

┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
│ Hackathon 2025                      │
│ 🎓 IIT Bombay (Other College)      │ ← Different campus
│ 👥 250 going                       │
└─────────────────────────────────────┘

Both events show clearly - no confusion!
