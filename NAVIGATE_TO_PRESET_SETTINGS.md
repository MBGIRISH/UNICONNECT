# 🧭 Step-by-Step: Navigate to Upload Preset Settings

## 📍 FROM DASHBOARD TO PRESET SETTINGS

### **STEP 1: Go to Settings**

1. **Look at the LEFT SIDEBAR** (dark blue area)
2. **Scroll down** if needed
3. **Find:** "Settings" (gear icon ⚙️)
4. **Click:** "Settings"

**OR**

1. **Look at the TOP RIGHT** of the page
2. **Find:** "Settings" dropdown menu
3. **Click:** "Settings"

---

### **STEP 2: Go to Upload Settings**

1. **After clicking Settings**, you'll see a menu
2. **Look for:** "Upload" or "Upload Settings"
3. **Click:** "Upload"

**Direct path:** Settings → Upload

---

### **STEP 3: Find Upload Presets**

1. **On the Upload settings page**, scroll down
2. **Look for section:** "Upload presets"
3. **You should see:** A list of presets or "Add upload preset" button

---

### **STEP 4: Open Your Preset**

1. **Find:** `uniconnect_uploads` in the list
2. **Click on it** (click the name or edit button)
3. **This opens the preset editor**

---

### **STEP 5: Find "General" Tab**

1. **In the preset editor**, look at the TOP of the page
2. **You should see TABS:**
   - **General** ← This is the tab!
   - Transform
   - Manage and Analyze
   - Optimize and Deliver
   - Advanced

3. **"General" should be selected by default** (highlighted in blue)

---

## 🎯 ALTERNATIVE: Direct Link Method

### **Quick Way:**

1. **Copy this URL:**
   ```
   https://cloudinary.com/console/settings/upload
   ```

2. **Paste in browser** and press Enter

3. **Scroll down** to "Upload presets"

4. **Click:** `uniconnect_uploads`

---

## 📋 COMPLETE NAVIGATION PATH

```
Dashboard
  ↓
Settings (left sidebar or top right)
  ↓
Upload (in settings menu)
  ↓
Scroll down to "Upload presets"
  ↓
Click "uniconnect_uploads"

You're now in preset editor
  ↓
"General" tab is at the top
```

- Title: "Edit Upload Preset 'uniconnect_uploads'"
