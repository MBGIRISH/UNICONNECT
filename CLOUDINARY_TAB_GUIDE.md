## ✅ **"Manage and Analyze" Tab (Current Tab)**

**Status:** ✅ **NO CHANGES NEEDED**

This tab is for **optional features**:
- Tags (optional metadata)
- Context (optional metadata)  
- Moderation (optional content filtering)
- Media Analysis (optional AI features)

**You can leave everything OFF/default** - these don't affect basic uploads!

---

## 🔴 **CRITICAL TABS TO CHECK:**

### **1. "General" Tab** ⭐ **MOST IMPORTANT!**

**Go to:** Click "General" in left sidebar

**MUST HAVE:**
- ✅ **Signing Mode:** `Unsigned` (NOT Signed!)
- ✅ **Folder:** `uniconnect` (or leave empty)

**If "Signing Mode" is "Signed":**
- ❌ Uploads will FAIL
- ✅ Change to "Unsigned" and Save

---

### **2. "Optimize and Deliver" Tab** ⭐ **IMPORTANT!**

**Go to:** Click "Optimize and Deliver" in left sidebar

**MUST HAVE:**
- ✅ **Access control:** `Public`
- ✅ **Format:** Leave empty
- ✅ **Allowed formats:** Leave empty

**If "Access control" is not "Public":**
- ❌ Files won't be accessible
- ✅ Change to "Public" and Save

---

### **3. "Transform" or "Advanced" Tab** ⭐ **IMPORTANT!**

**Go to:** Click "Transform" or "Advanced" in left sidebar

**MUST HAVE:**
- ✅ **Resource type:** `Auto` (or enable Image, Video, Raw separately)

**If Resource type is restricted:**
- ❌ Some file types won't upload
- ✅ Set to "Auto" and Save
